My Projects

ICT Major Project

Developed a platform for salons ‘Band of Barbers’ that connects hair salons and customers together on one platform, which allows the hair salons to register within it. Salon owner can register the staff details and update the availabilities and working hours, which helps customers in booking appointments. Accordingly, customers can book the appointments with different hairstylists from different salons. The platform provides easy user interface and delivers the services effectively and efficiently.
